Noun

βῐ́βλος • (bíblos) f (genitive βῐ́βλου); second declension

  1. Attic form of βῠ́βλος (búblos)

Declension

Second declension of ἡ βῐ́βλος; τῆς βῐ́βλου (Attic)

Derived terms

  • βίβλινος (bíblinos)
  • βιβλίον (biblíon)

Descendants

  • English: Bible
  • Greek: Βίβλος (Vívlos)
